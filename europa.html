Fun Facts

  • The surface of Europa is bombarded by radiation from Jupiters magnetosphere. Standing on the surface of Europa for a day would be almost certainly fatal.
  • Scientists believe Europa has a subsurface ocean, possibly capable of harboring life.
  • Europa has a thin atmosphere made up mostly of oxygen and water vapor.
