Iapetus

Planet: Saturn

Size (diameter): 1,469 Kilometers

Day Length: 79 Earth Days

Tidally Locked: Yes

Who discovered it: G. D. Cassini

Fun fact: Iapetus is named after the Titan Iapetus from Greek mythology and was the 6th extraterrestrial moon to be discovered.
